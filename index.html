About Bhardwaj Hospital

Your trusted multispeciality hospital in Varanasi, offering comprehensive medical services and expert care for all patients.

Bhardwaj Hospital, located in the Lahartara Bauliya area of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, has been a cornerstone of healthcare in the region for over 18 years. Renowned for its blend of modern medical practices and traditional Ayurvedic treatments, the hospital offers a comprehensive range of services across various specialties.
